# 🐾 virtual desktop pet 🐾

**meet your new virtual workspace buddy!** a cute cat that wanders, walks, sits, and naps right on top of your screen as you work. plus, you can grab and drag it anywhere you want!

### features:
🐈 **cute animations:** walking, sitting, and napping  
🖱️ **interactive:** grab and drag your pet to any corner of your screen  
💻 **always on top:** keeps you company while you work or browse
